Drink plenty water - 6 to 8 glasses a day. Women who are breastfeeding should drink the amount needed to satisfy their thirst. Water, milk products and unsweetened juices are the best choices. Limit tea and coffee to in-between meals in order not to interfere too much with iron absorption. Try to avoid excessive amounts of lemonade or drinks with a high sugar content, especially if you are gaining too much weight.
